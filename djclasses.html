BECOME A TRUE DJ: LEARN WITH VINYL

Learn the real craft of DJing with vinyl records, just like the legends. Under the guidance of Javi Beat DJ, resident DJ at iconic Dubai venues such as Aura Skypool, Drift Beach Club, Azure Beach Club, Ammos Greek Restaurant, and Attiko, you will master the skills that define a true DJ.

The course includes:

  1. Understanding turntables and vinyl records.
  2. Manual beatmatching and transitions.
  3. EQ and filter techniques for smooth mixing.
  4. Introduction to scratch techniques.

Course Duration & Flexibility

The full course consists of **20 hours** of training, structured in **10 sessions of 2 hours each**. However, we understand that every student has different learning needs and schedules, so we offer **flexible timing** to adapt to your availability.

Whether you're a complete beginner or already have some DJ experience, we personalize each lesson to match your skill level and progress. Our goal is to ensure that every student **feels confident mixing with vinyl and performing live** by the end of the course.

By the end of this course, you will have mastered **turntable techniques, seamless mixing skills, and the essential art of reading a crowd**. You will also gain **exclusive professional insights** into the Dubai luxury scene, learning how to curate the perfect "Sunset Vibes" and high-end atmosphere that the world's most elite venues demand.

Who Is This Course For?

✅ Anyone passionate about DJing and looking to learn the **authentic way with vinyl**.
✅ Beginners who want to start from scratch with **personalized guidance**.
✅ DJs who already mix digitally and want to expand their skills into **vinyl mixing and live performances**.
