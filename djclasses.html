BECOME A TRUE DJ: LEARN WITH VINYL

Learn the real craft of DJing with vinyl records, just like the legends. Under the guidance of Javi Beat DJ and Sergio Trumpet & DJ, resident DJs at iconic Dubai venues such as Aura Skypool, Drift Beach Club, Azure Beach Club, Ammos Greek Restaurant, and Attiko, you will master the skills that define a true DJ.

These classes also feature guest sessions with Sergio Trumpet & DJ, who will introduce the basics of Ableton Live for creating live music performances, providing a well-rounded learning experience for aspiring DJs and producers.

The course includes:

  1. Understanding turntables and vinyl records.
  2. Manual beatmatching and transitions.
  3. EQ and filter techniques for smooth mixing.
  4. Introduction to scratch techniques.
  5. Special Ableton Live session for LIVE basics.
