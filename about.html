BIOGRAPHY

THE SONIC
ARCHITECT

Javi Beat is a versatile international DJ currently based in Dubai. Beginning his journey in 1999, his roots are deeply embedded in the sounds of the '80s and the analog warmth of Hip-Hop and Scratch culture.

Years of collecting vinyl records built a pristine foundation for his craft. Today, he translates that analog sensibility into meticulously curated, high-end digital sets. As a resident DJ at Drift Beach Club and AURA Skypool, he brings a refined, dynamic energy to every performance, effortlessly switching genres to elevate the atmosphere of luxury beach clubs and exclusive events.
